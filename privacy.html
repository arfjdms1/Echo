Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how Echo, a Discord chatbot developed and maintained by Aarav Khemka, collects, uses, and protects user data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Echo, the following types of data may be collected and stored:

  • User Identifiers: Discord user ID, username, discriminator, and server IDs.
  • Message Content: Text you send to the bot, including commands and responses.
  • Contextual Metadata: Channel IDs, timestamps, and message IDs relevant to your interactions.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect this data for the following purposes:

  • To enable core functionalities such as maintaining conversation history and contextual responses.
  • To enhance and improve the bot’s performance and features over time.
  • To provide server administrators insights or moderation tools if enabled.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely in a private database environment, protected by access controls and encrypted communication. Only authorized personnel (e.g., the developer) have access to this data.

Data will be retained only for as long as needed to support bot functionality, unless otherwise required by legal obligations or user request.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your data will never be sold, rented, or shared with third parties. However, data may be disclosed under the following circumstances:

  • When required to comply with legal obligations, such as law enforcement requests.
  • When necessary to protect our legal rights or ensure user safety.

5. User Control and Rights

You may request to:

  • Access your stored chat history.
  • Delete your data from our systems.
  • Opt out of data collection (note: this may limit certain bot features).

To make a request, please email: aaravkhemka@dreamweaver-ai.xyz

6. Use by Minors

This bot is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. If you believe a minor has used the bot in violation of this policy, please contact us to ensure appropriate data deletion.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be clearly communicated, and the “Effective Date” will be revised accordingly. Continued use of the bot signifies acceptance of any modifications.

8. Contact
